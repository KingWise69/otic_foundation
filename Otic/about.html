about us

What is Otic?

Otic Foundation is a Ugandan social enterprise at the forefront of leveraging the immense power of Artificial Intelligence (AI) to shape a better future. • We recognize the fast-paced growth of this emerging technology and see the need to create an infrastructure that will support AI technology to thrive and solve problems in the Ugandan context.

Moving forward, we aim to expand this initiative to all educational institutions in Uganda, impacting over 20,000 students by the end of 2024.

Our Core Values

Innovation: We foster a culture of innovation, constantly pushing the boundaries of AI capabilities. Ethical Integrity: We adhere to the highest ethical standards, ensuring fairness, transparency, and accountability in every aspect of our work.

Collaboration: We actively seek collaborations with research institutions, nonprofit organizations, industry leaders, and policymakers, fostering interdisciplinary cooperation and knowledge sharing.

Impact Orientation: We employ rigorous evaluation methodologies, data-driven insights, and feedback loops to continuously refine and improve our strategies, ensuring that our endeavours have a meaningful and lasting societal impact.

Collaboration and Networking
• Industry Partnerships: Apply AI solutions across sectors.

Our Current and Planned Impact

• We have empowered over 600 students across six higher learning institutions to become influential participants in the Fourth Industrial Revolution.
• We have introduced them to the transformative potential of AI in critical sectors such as agriculture, healthcare, and finance, which are particularly relevant to Uganda.
• Moving forward, we aim to expand this initiative to all educational institutions in Uganda, impacting over 20,000 students by the end of 2024.
