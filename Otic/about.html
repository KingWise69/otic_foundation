About Us

What is the Otic Foundation?

Otic Foundation is a Ugandan social enterprise at the forefront of leveraging the immense power of Artificial Intelligence (AI) to shape a better future.

We recognize the fast-paced growth of this emerging technology and see the need to create an infrastructure that will support AI technology to thrive and solve problems in the Ugandan context.

Our Core Values

Innovation: We foster a culture of innovation, constantly pushing the boundaries of AI capabilities.

Ethical Integrity: We adhere to the highest ethical standards, ensuring fairness, transparency, and accountability in every aspect of our work.

Collaboration: We actively seek collaborations with research institutions, nonprofit organizations, industry leaders, and policymakers, fostering interdisciplinary cooperation and knowledge sharing.

Impact Orientation: We employ rigorous evaluation methodologies, data-driven insights, and feedback loops to continuously refine and improve our strategies, ensuring that our endeavours have a meaningful and lasting societal impact.
